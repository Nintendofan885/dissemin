{% trans "Green Open Access" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Many researchers do not use their right to make their papers freely available online, in addition to the paywalled version offered by traditional publishers. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} There are two majer advantages of unpaywalling your papers: {% endblocktrans %}

  1. {% blocktrans trimmed %} Your research is available to everyone. This increases your readership and makes your research more acknowledged, because your research better spreads. This effect is measurable with the so called Open Access Citation Advantage. {% endblocktrans %}
  2. {% blocktrans trimmed %} Libraries do need to buy overpriced electronic subscriptions to journals, when they can afford them at all. {% endblocktrans %}
