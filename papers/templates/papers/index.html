Green Open Access

Many researchers do not use their right to make their papers freely available online, in addition to the paywalled version offered by traditional publishers.

There are two majer advantages of unpaywalling your papers:

  1. Your research is available to everyone. This increases your readership and makes your research more acknowledged, because your research better spreads. This effect is measurable with the so called Open Access Citation Advantage.
  2. Libraries do need to by overpriced electronic subscriptions to journals, when they can afford them at all.

Open Repositories

Uploading your papers on your own webpage is not enough. Such copies are less stable and harder to find than document uploaded to well-indexed repositories such as arXiv, Zenodo and institutional repositories.

Dissemin for Libraries

As library you can use Dissemin to find publications of your researchers that are behind a paywall. Dissemin can upload publications directly into your institutional repository and tries to support your local worklfow as much as possible.

In addition you can advertise Dissemin to your researchers and show them, how easy it is to liberate their publications!
